# 10 Mini Crosswords

Ten 5×5 minis drawn from a **1,574-entry clue bank**. Every across and down entry is a real word, every one of the **100 answers is unique across all ten puzzles** — no repeats within a grid or between grids — and all ten load cleanly through the player's own validator.

Grids are fully checked (every square belongs to both an across and a down answer) with 180° rotational symmetry, and use five different block shapes. Blocks are shown as ■.
